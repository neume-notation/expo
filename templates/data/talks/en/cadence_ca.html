Although we can no longer tell how these melodies sounded, we can tell some things about how they worked. One of the key elements of a piece of music is when you stop, and take a breath, and pause, and then start again, at a cadence. This happens at the end of a sentence, or at the end of a clause, or a smaller unit of text. Of course, there is always a cadence at the end of a chant.

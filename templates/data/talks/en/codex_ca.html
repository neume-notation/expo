Just one page of this manuscript survives. It has writing on both sides. Some of its notation has been trimmed off the side, and this tells us that the parchment was originally bigger. Another whole page very likely existed on the left hand side of this manuscript. You can see some stitching holes on one side. They are not from the original manuscript because they cover up the initial. They are more likely from a later binding, when this fragment served as a flyleaf.
